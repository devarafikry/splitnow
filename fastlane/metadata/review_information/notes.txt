SplitNow is a single-purpose split-bill app. No accounts, no groups,
no IOUs.

Golden path the reviewer can exercise:

1. Tap "+ New split" on Home.
2. EITHER tap "Scan receipt" + grant camera (or use photo library),
   OR tap "Skip" — both paths reach Describe.
3. On Describe, type something like: "Anna had pasta, Bob had pizza,
   we shared garlic bread." Tap Continue.
4. The app sends the OCR text (if any) + the typed sentence to
   Google Gemini. Gemini returns a structured split (per-person
   items + shared + charges).
5. On Review, tap "Share split" to render a receipt-card image and
   open the iOS share sheet.

Denying camera and photo permissions does not break the app — the
typed-only flow is fully supported.

The app uses standard HTTPS only (Gemini API). No tracking, no
accounts, no login.
