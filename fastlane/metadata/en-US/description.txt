Split the bill, keep it simple.

SplitNow is a single-purpose split-bill app. No accounts, no
groups, no IOUs, no balances. Scan a receipt, type freely who
ordered what, and the AI does the rest. Share a receipt-card image
with payment details in two taps.

• Scan or skip. Point your camera at the receipt — on-device OCR
  pulls the items. Or skip and type from scratch. Either way works.
• Plain-English describe. "Anna had the pasta, Bob had pizza, we
  shared garlic bread." That's the whole input. AI sorts it.
• Tax + tip split modes. Proportional, equal, or skip. Pick once,
  SplitNow remembers.
• Multi-currency, IDR default. Detects the receipt's currency
  automatically; override in Settings if needed.
• Share a receipt card. One image, one share sheet. Send it to
  the group chat and you're done.
• Bank + e-wallet + QRIS. Add your payment methods once; they
  embed on the share card so people know exactly how to pay you.
• No accounts. No login. No analytics. The receipt photo never
  leaves your phone — only the OCR text and your description do.
