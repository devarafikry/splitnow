Three steps, ten seconds.

Most split-bill apps make you add people, set up groups, settle balances. SplitNow does none of that. Each receipt is its own thing — scan, share, done.

1

Scan the receipt

Use your camera. On-device OCR pulls out the items. Apple Vision Kit on iOS, ML Kit on Android — nothing leaves your phone for OCR.

2

Type what happened

"Budi got nasi goreng pedas, Tina ordered seafood, Joko only had air putih." Free-text. AI figures out who owes what.

3

Share the split

Per-person amounts on a receipt card with your bank/QRIS info baked in. Send to WhatsApp, LINE, anywhere. Friends pay you directly.

Just enough, nothing more.

The category is full of apps that want to be your social graph and your bank. SplitNow stays out of the way.

No login

Open the app, scan, split. Nothing to remember, nothing to recover. Your data lives on your phone.

One receipt at a time

No groups, no household, no running balance. Each split is finished when you share the image.

Indonesian bank-aware

BCA, Mandiri, Jago, GoPay, OVO, DANA, ShopeePay, QRIS. Pick which one to embed on the share card.

Multi-currency

IDR default. USD, SGD, MYR, JPY, EUR, GBP. Numbers stay clean either way.

AI-assisted, not AI-dependent

Every item is editable inline. The model gets you 90% there in 2 seconds; you fix the last 10% if you want.

Light + dark

Designed for both. Coral pops in light mode, glows in dark mode. Matches your system.
